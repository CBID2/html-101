Queen

Queen are a British rock band formed in 1970 in London, influenced by progressive rock, hard rock and heavy metal,
but transitioned into more mainstream themes in arena rock and pop rock.
They are one of the world's best selling music artists.

The artists are:

  • Freddie Mercury
  • Brian May
  • Roger Taylor
  • John Deacon
